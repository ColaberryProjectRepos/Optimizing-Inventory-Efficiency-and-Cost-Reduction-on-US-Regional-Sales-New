Project Overview 1

In the competitive world of supply chain management, optimizing inventory efficiency is crucial to reducing costs and increasing profitability. Despite advancements in technology and logistics, many companies still face challenges such as inefficient inventory management, stockouts, and excess inventory. These inefficiencies not only inflate holding costs but also result in missed sales opportunities. By leveraging advanced data science techniques, this project focuses on analyzing and optimizing procurement processes and marketing strategies across the company's warehouse network. Through detailed analysis of sales, inventory, and order quantities, we aim to uncover patterns, streamline operations, and provide actionable insights. This project will enhance inventory efficiency, boost profit margins, and improve overall supply chain performance, positioning the company for greater success in the industry.
